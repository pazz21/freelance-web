Quality : Philosophy

At BRVS Group we believe that quality is a culture, expressed in the way we interact with and cater to the needs of our customers, and in the products and processes we deliver to them. So while we have met several external quality parameters, we are even more stringent about setting and meeting our own exacting standards.

At BRVS Group, Quality is a continuous process that is reflected in the Company's emphasis on making every Associate in the company part of the Quality processes and methodologies.

The BRVS Group is an avowedly employee-oriented organization. We firmly believe in the principle of people before technology, which is why our greatest asset is our people. Our people are handpicked from the best in the industry and we provide them with a challenging and invigorating work environment that is conducive to excellence. Which is to say, that we provide our people with all the facilities that they would need to deliver the highest quality at all times, investing in on-going training and rewarding real performance.

BRVS Group is an organization that is young at heart. Our employee profile constitutes functional and technical consultants with extensive industry and solution implementation experience and skills honed through continuous training programs. Our flexible management style promotes a high degree of responsibility, participation and opportunity for growth. Our philosophy is to be value-based and process-driven and we encourage our consultants to be self-motivated and action-oriented.

All this is a reflection of our strong recruiting program. All our clients have repeatedly complimented us on our outstanding recruiting program that truly is the backbone of our company. This stems from the belief that when you hire exceptional talent you create an exceptional company.

Training & Development

Since individual and group achievements are so important at BRVS Group, we also invest substantial time and money in providing valuable training to our people. Training programs range from classroom instructions to hands-on and template-based training and skill building programs. We provide our employees with an extensive knowledge bank of industry information and professional support form across the world.

At BRVS Group we encourage our consultants to be leaders, and continuous leadership needs continuous encouragement. We take advantage of every opportunity to encourage and direct talent, potential and initiative.

Training

BRVS Group recognizes our clients need to enhance the technical knowledge of their own employees. BRVS Group offers training services designed to support clients' end-users during major new deployments, to support technical service personnel in the installation and servicing of desktop and network environments, to educate telecommunication technicians on implementation and support of various technologies, and train developers in the latest programming languages and platforms. Many of these services can be provided at the client's site(s) or at a third-party location. Our flexible course hours are designed to minimize any disruption to a client's operations.

Going beyond traditional standards, BRVS Group' training courses include pre-class and post-class knowledge assessments which are compared to client-defined learning objectives. These objectives are aligned with specific lectures, demonstrations, hands-on exercises, and sample testing known to successfully transfer knowledge and build technical skill. BRVS Group currently maintains an excellent overall certification pass rate.

BRVS Group provides Instructor-Led Training (ILT) that is tailored specifically toward technical changes made within an organization. Our training curriculums are centered on leading edge products and services with a focus on employee performance improvement through effective and measurable transfer of knowledge.
